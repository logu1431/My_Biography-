My Hobbies

Music is my constant companion, helping me relax and focus. Whether on the field or in front of a screen, I always find joy in my hobbies. These activities shape my lifestyle and keep me motivated.

My Favourite Story - Mahabharatham

The Mahabharatham is my favourite story because it is full of lessons about life, dharma, and justice. It teaches about the consequences of greed, pride, and betrayal. The story’s characters are complex and human, showing both strengths and flaws. It inspires me with tales of bravery and sacrifice. The epic continues to influence how I see life’s challenges.
